Adobe Flash, the legendary graphic software, has been discounted

Adobe Flash Player, Adobe's legendary multimedia software platform, has been discounted 28 years after it's release due to engine limitations and security concerns. Alternatives such as HTML5 have been on the market for years now, and browser-based game developer/publishers have mostly ported their games into HTML5 instead of Flash Player.
Steve Jobs was one of the first people who called out Flash Player as not secure. He disapproved the usage of Flash Player in iOS phones.
HTML5 comes with pretty much every web browser nowadays, so playing browser-based games should not require an additional setup in these days. Even mobile phones use them, so playing browser-based games on mobiles is possible, and has been for years.
