AMD RX 6800/XT versions are released finally, sold out in seconds as expected

12/18/20
AMD's flagship graphics card, RX 6800 and XT versions of it are released today. The enthusiast card, RX 6900 XT was released 10 days ago, in 8th of December.
All of the cards were sold out in seconds, hoarders have bought them all with the help of bots.

Some benchmarks of them are:
The Division 2 benchmark is somewhat disappointing, RTX 3080, which is cheaper than RX 6900 XT, performs better than latter card.
Lastly, prices of three cards are:
Their flagship card, RX 6800's MSRP is 579$
Their high end card, RX 6800 XT's MSRP is 649$
Finally, their enthusiast card, RX 6900 XT's MSRP is 1000$.
