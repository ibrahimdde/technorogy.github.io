Thodex, a Turkish crypto-currency trading site's owner fled with company's money

04/23/21
Thodex's owner, Faruk Fatih Ozer, fled Turkey with 2 billion dollars, which was collected by users who deposited money to the app for crypto-currency trading. The app itself was widely used amongst crypto-traders. It had 2 million users. The details are still not clear, and the guy is still at large. He was last spotted at Albania.

His brother was detained by Turkish police in Turkey for investigation.
