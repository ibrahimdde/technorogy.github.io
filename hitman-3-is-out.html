Hitman 3 is out

01/20/21
Hitman 3, IO Interactive's newest game is out. In the game, you have to kill some people under specific conditions. Game will be exclusive for Epic Store for 12 months, after that other sellers will be able to sell the game on their platforms. One of the major game pirating groups, CODEX, have pirated the game on the release date. You can buy the game from Epic Store for 60$.
Some benchmarks of this game with different graphical cards of are:

Despite the graphics, game isn't that demanding, getting 60 fps in 1080p medium settings will not be a big hassle. A card like RX 580 or GTX 1060 can get 60 fps on this game, even in populated areas.
Getting 60 fps from 1080p ultra settings will require a nice graphics card, a GTX 1070 ti or AMD equivalent will give you a nice experience in 1080p ultra settings.

Benchmarks are from Tomshardware.
